Level.Pathfinder

AI-powered strategic intelligence that surfaces signals that matter.

Pathfinder combines first-party and third-party data with search, social, and competitive behavior signals to rapidly build audience segments, personas, and journey maps. AI surfaces the patterns. Humans shape the meaning.

From raw signals to actionable audience truth

Static demographic research doesn't win markets. Pathfinder combines 1P and 3P data with search, social, and competitive signals, giving strategists the foundation to build audience segments and journey maps grounded in real behavior, not assumptions.

The Pathfinder Process

⚡

1. AI surfaces the signals

Ingests 1P and 3P data, analyzing search, social, and competitive behavior to detect actionable market patterns at speed.

🧠

🎯

What Pathfinder Builds

High-fidelity personas

We move beyond basic demographics to define the exact emotional drivers, financial pressures, top intents, and content frameworks required to convert specific sub-segments.

Example

"Debt Reshaper"

Driver: Stress Reduction

The "Debt Reshaper" sub-segment are homeowners under mounting financial pressure who are actively seeking relief, not leverage. They are typically mid-life homeowners with meaningful home equity and growing unsecured debt.

They are not motivated by optionality or financial optimization. They are motivated by stress reduction, control, and dignity.

Level.Signal

Focus your investment on the leads that actually move.

Built in partnership with Google, Signal is an AI-powered propensity model that predicts which leads are ready for the next revenue-driving action. It scores real behavior, assigns value, and activates across media, CRM, and lifecycle for immediate impact.

Predictive lead scoring powered by behavioral intelligence

Signal learns from the behavioral data that actually reveals intent, not vanity metrics. It reads how people move through your site, how they interact with media, and what they share through forms and your CRM. From that mix of behavior and context, it predicts who is ready for the next revenue-driving action and pushes that score into the platforms that need it.
